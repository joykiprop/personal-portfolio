Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 100g pancetta
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti.
  2. Fry pancetta.
  3. Mix eggs with cheese.
  4. Combine everything.
