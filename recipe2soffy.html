Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef (sirloin or tenderloin), sliced thinly
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Cooked pasta or rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add beef slices and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in mushrooms and cook for another 3 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle flour and paprika over the mixture, stir well.
  5. Pour in beef broth and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Lower the heat and add heavy cream and sour cream. Stir until smooth.
  7. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot over pasta or rice!
