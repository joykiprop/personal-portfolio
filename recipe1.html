Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions in oil until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook until tender.
  5. Mix in yogurt and let it simmer.
  6. In another pot, cook the soaked rice until it's 80% done.
  7. Layer the rice over the chicken mixture and cover tightly.
  8. Let it steam on low heat for 15 minutes.
  9. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot!
